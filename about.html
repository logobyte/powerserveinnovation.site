What We Do

Allow Power Serve Innovation to help you get clean, safe, and renewable energy.

CONSULTATION

At Power Serve Innovation, we ensure that your investment is worthwhile. We conduct thorough consultations for various establishments, including residential, commercial, industrial, and agricultural, to provide the best and most suitable solar packages.

SOLAR INSTALLATION

Power Serve Innovation provides top-notch, excellent-quality work. We handle everything from brand-carry panels and inverters to design, planning, mounting on PV packages, and testing of PV modules.

NET-METERING SERVICES

Our net-metering services enable customers of Distribution Utilities (DUs) to install on-site Renewable Energy (RE) facilities not exceeding 100 kilowatts (kW) in capacity. This allows them to generate electricity for their own use, with excess electricity automatically exported to the DU's distribution system.

REMODELLING & CLEANING

Power Serve Innovation offers professional services, including reinstallation and remodeling of PV panels, cleaning maintenance, Wi-Fi configuration, railing and wire maintenance, redesigning and upgrading, and roofing realignment.

About Us

We take exceptional pride in all our work from preliminary design through permitting installation, final inspection and interconnection with the utility company. Here in Power Serve Innovation all our personnel, design and installation teams comprise of enthusiastic people who share a common goal of using solar energy for a brighter future for all.

Our Services

WHAT WE DO? Power Serve Innovation offers complete installation Services from Panel installation to Inverter power-up This is your guild thru our Service flow.

1

CONSULTATION

Our PSI Sales associates will give you an insight into what Solar PV systems are. They will ask for your electric bill details and give you an affordable package that fits your preferred brand. They will also assist you with your concerns and set you up for an ocular visit. Our Sales Associates are dedicated to serving and helping you with your Solar PV System.

Read More
2

OCULAR VISIT

Our Technical Sales will explain everything about Solar Systems and what systems are fitted based on your Roofing, Electrical Consumption, and Distribution Utility (e.g. Meralco/Panel/Batelco) they'll also inform you about the Proper use of our PV System and how can we save More. Our Technical sales are very flexible and knowledgeable in their field.

Read More
3

ROOFING DESIGN

After the ocular visit, our Junior Graphic Designer and Technical Sales will make a design of the roofing, panel, and inverter that you have chosen. To make this design our Technical Sales consultant performs a drone shot on your roofing to capture the actual size and length of your roof while they are in site. The graphic designer will make a layout of the proposed plan to give you more idea of what your roof looks like when the solar panels are installed.

Read More
4

INSTALLATION

After the Roofing Design, our sales team will prepare all the materials and deliver them to our client, which will then be installed by our experienced installer who will place the solar PV systems.

Read More
5

POWER UP

After the Installation of panels, our Technical Associates are the ones authorized to power up your Inverter connected to the panel. To avoid electrical damage and accidents we want to assure you that we want the best and excellent quality installation for our client.

Read More
6

COMMISION AND TESTING

Once our Technical Associate conducted power on your system, our Technical Head will check the system to see if the standards installation was followed by our TA, and it also checks the materials to see if they are all durable. We at PSI want our clients to enjoy their investments in saving more energy.
